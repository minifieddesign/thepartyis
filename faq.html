Three parties, you say?

Well, it works this way: The Wedding will be a small affair in distant lands, but we still want to celebrate with as many of you as we can. We'd love to have you join us when and where you're able to.

Our main reception will be an evening party in June on the Willamette River in Portland.

And still, we know that a few will be unable to make it, some who have had far too much a hand in her upbringing to miss out on the excitement, so we’ll be headed to Grand Rapids in late October.

Why Copenhagen?

It's often the first thing people ask us when we tell them where we’re getting married. We decided early on that travel was something we wanted to pursue together, as a couple, a joint endeavor, always looking to have something on the books, the next destination to plan for and explore. So it was only natural to want to celebrate the big day in a new city far away.
